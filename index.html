ABOUT REACTIVE MIND AI

Our Mission

Reactive Mind AI was founded in 2024 with a singular vision: make powerful artificial intelligence accessible to every personal computer user, for free. We believe AI should run locally, respect privacy, and empower individuals — not extract their data.

Our Story

Born from a frustration with cloud-dependent AI tools that compromise privacy and charge high fees, Reactive Mind AI started as an open-source project. Today, we serve over 500,000 developers, writers, and professionals worldwide — all using AI locally on their own machines.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: Your data stays on your device. No telemetry, no tracking.
  • Open Access: Core features are free forever. AI should be a right, not a luxury.
  • Community Driven: Built with feedback from our 500K+ users.
  • Continuous Innovation: New models and features shipped every month.
